AfriLabs Terminal

Welcome to the AfriLabs Terminal, a portal for discovering opportunities for investment or social impact in Africa. This data that is monitored, mined and made available by the team at AfriLabs. We take a different approach to sharing data about early stage-African investment opportunities by considering the environments the companies operate in, in addition to traditional performance indicators.

Why should you use the AfriLabs Terminal?

Vetted Information

Rather than rely on self-reported information from the companies themselves, the AfriLabs Terminal only contains information that was researched by our staff. MAking sure the information is accurate saves investors doing research a great deal of time.

Risk Profile

Africa has many diverse cities and countries. Our profile of each company considers the context in which they operate. At a glance, you can determine to the risk associated with each venture

Qualified Leads

Because we pre-screen startups before they appear here, funders can trust that a level of due-diligence has been conducted on every listed venture. This is meant to help you focus on only the best opportunities.
